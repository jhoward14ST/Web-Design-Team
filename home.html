Welcome

Pure Rae Cooking takes a balanced approach to clean eating with an emphasis on whole grains, lean proteins, and seasonal vegetables. We are passionate about serving healthy, satisfying food inspired by global cuisine.

Services

Meal Prep

Description

Our most popular service. We determine your diet and preferences to build a custom meal plan. After creating a custom menu your meals are prepared in a professional kitchen and promptly delivered to your door.

Accomodating for requirements such as:

  • Vegan
  • Gluten-free
  • Kosher
  • Keto
  • Low glycemic
  • Low carb
  • Allergies

Catering

Description

We offer catering to smaller intimate events where logistics don’t get in the way of delivering quality. After understanding your needs we will create a custom menu for your event and deliver a plated or spread style meal.

Catering to events such as:

  • Private Dinner Parties
  • Baby showers
  • Birthdays
  • Family Get Togethers
  • Anniversaries
  • Corporate events
  • Brunch Social
