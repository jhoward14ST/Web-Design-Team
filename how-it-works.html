HOW IT WORKS

Pure Rae Cooking takes a balanced approach to clean eating with an emphasis on whole grains, lean proteins, and seasonal vegetables. We are passionate about serving healthy, satisfying food inspired by global cuisine.

A CUSTOM MENU IS CREATED

The first step in creating a delicious meal plan starts with a session to figure out what your dietary needs are and getting a feel for your plate. From here, a custom meal plan is created and the preparation begins!

LE CORDON BLEU DILIGENCE

Your meals are expertly crafted in a professional kitchen with the finest locally sourced ingredients.

DELIVERD TO YOUR DOORSTEP

Once your meals are ready, they will be delivered to your doorstep in tote bags that keep them fresh for hours after delivery in case you arent home.
